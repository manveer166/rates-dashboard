## Form-builder setup tips

**For Google Forms:**

1. Create a new form
2. Settings → Responses → toggle ON "Collect email addresses" (set to "Verified" so it pre-fills from their Google account)
3. Settings → Responses → "Send respondents a copy of their response" — useful
4. For each question above, use the type indicated *(Linear scale | Multiple choice | Short answer | Long text)*
5. Mark required questions with the asterisk toggle
6. Use Page breaks between Section 1–9 to keep it scannable

**For Typeform** (if you want it prettier):

1. Map question types: Rating → Opinion Scale, Multiple choice → Multiple Choice, Short answer → Short Text, Long text → Long Text, 0–10 NPS → NPS block
2. Use Welcome screen for the intro
3. Use a Thank You screen at the end
4. Logic jumps: skip Q6.2 + Q6.3 if Q6.1 = "Didn't open it"

**Send protocol** (per the day-12 timing in the second email):

> Subject: Macro Manv beta — 20 mins of feedback (and the Founding Seat offer)
>
> Hi [NAME], two days left in the beta. Here's the structured form I promised — about 20 minutes, every question matters. Even the "this is useless" answers are useful.
>
> [FORM LINK]
>
> Once you submit I'll have your Founding Seat offer ready within 24 hours.

**After all submissions are in:**

1. Export Google Form responses to Sheets
2. Calculate per-section average rating
3. Open-text answers: dump them into one doc, look for recurring themes (use Claude or similar to summarise if you want)
4. Aggregate themes back to the cohort in an email — shows you took the feedback seriously and builds goodwill before the Founding payment moment
